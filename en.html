GDRTC

广东省无线电公共技术服务中心

Guangdong Provincial Radio Public Technology Service Center(GDRTC)has been authorized as the testing laboratory of China radio transmission equipment Type Approval since 2010, covers an area of 2000 square meters. It possesses advanced and comprehensive radio equipment RF testing laboratories with 7 shielding rooms and 3m fully-anechoic chamber. With enormous experience in radio equipment management and testing field, perfect technical ability and experienced technicians, GDRTC has gained the accreditation by China National Accreditation Service for Conformity Assessment(CNAS). It committed to the business of SRRC, testing service, international certification, aiming to establish a public technology service platform which acrosses South China including Guangdong Province, Hong Kong and Macao in radio industry.
